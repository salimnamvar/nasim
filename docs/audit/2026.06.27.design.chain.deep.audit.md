### CAR-SM-03: Initial and Terminal States

**Challenge:** "Initial state shown as `[*] --> first_real_state`. Terminal state(s) clearly marked."

**Action:** Checked state machine entry/exit.

**Result:** ⚠️ MINOR ISSUE
- Initial: `[*] --> IDLE : AGT-01` — ✅ correct
- Terminal: `IDLE --> [*] : API-06` — ⚠️ ISSUE: IDLE has an outgoing transition to `[*]` (terminal) labeled API-06. But IDLE also has outgoing transitions to LISTENING, SERVING, PLANNING. A state with outgoing transitions to non-terminal states cannot also be a terminal state. The `IDLE --> [*]` transition implies the agent can shut down from IDLE, which is valid, but the dual role (active state + potential terminal) is unusual and may confuse readers.

**Severity:** LOW — technically valid but semantically ambiguous. Consider a separate TERMINATED state.
